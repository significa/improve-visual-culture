# Monthly Exercises
## Reverse-Engineering Practice

These exercises reinforce everything learned in the previous phases.

They are not about aesthetics or redesign.
They are about extracting **visual systems**, understanding **constraints**, and developing **visual judgment** through deliberate practice.

Complete **one exercise per month**.
